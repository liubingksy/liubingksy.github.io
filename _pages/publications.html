About My Research

My research centers on the quantitative discovery of temporal, spatial, and network-based patterns in large-scale dynamic systems. I am particularly fascinated by two complex systems: the evolution of scientific knowledge and urban mobility. My future work will continue to explore knowledge flow and knowledge dynamics—understanding how knowledge moves across regions and communities to drive innovation.
