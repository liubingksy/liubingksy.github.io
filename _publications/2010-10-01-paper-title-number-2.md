**Highlights**:
- This research conducts a spatial–temporal analysis of real-time navigation data.
- This research reveals four typical traffic accessibility evolution patterns.
- A new indicator is introduced to resemble the spin direction in the Ising model.
- Phase transition-like phenomena are identified through the phase diagrams.
